Behind the Collection

From concept to creation

01
Phase One

Concept & Sketching

Every collection starts with a blank page. We sketch dozens of silhouettes, refining each line until it feels honest.

02
Phase Two

Material Selection

We choose the fabric before the final sketch. Deadstock wool, organic cotton, responsible leather — the material dictates the design.

03
Phase Three

Sampling & Fitting

Each piece goes through three fit rounds on real bodies. We adjust, refine, and test until it moves exactly right.

04
Phase Four

Final Production

Small batch production with trusted partners. Every stitch inspected, every finish considered.
