Process

From concept to launch

How each collection comes to life — eight steps, one philosophy.

Step 01

Concept

Every collection starts with a core idea — a feeling, a silhouette, a fabric.

Step 02

Mood Board

We gather inspiration — textures, colors, and reference points that define the edit.

Step 03

Fabric Selection

Material comes first — deadstock wool, organic cotton, responsible leather.

Step 04

Sketches

Designs are drawn and refined, each line serving a purpose.

Step 05

Tailoring

Three fit rounds on real bodies — adjust, refine, perfect.

Step 06

Quality Check

Every stitch inspected, every finish considered.

Step 07
